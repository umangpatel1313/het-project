Nike's Next Step: Custom & Sustainable Footwear

Innovation meets eco-conscious design through AI and 3D scanning

Executive Summary

Nike is launching custom-fit, eco-friendly shoes using 3D scanning and AI. These shoes are tailored to individuals and built from sustainable materials, appealing to consumers who value both personalization and environmental impact. With a growing footwear market expected to exceed $725 billion, Nike aims to lead the way in innovation.

Product Strategy

Using 3D foot scans and AI, Nike will create unique, sustainable shoes tailored for each individual.

Product Line

  • Nike FitPro: $200 – Custom running shoes (3D + eco)
  • Nike EcoTrainer: $180 – Training shoes (AI design + recycled)
  • Nike GreenStep: $150 – Daily wear (sustainable, comfortable)

Target Market & Value

Targeted at 18–45 year-olds who care about health, tech, and the planet. These shoes offer comfort, personalization, and green innovation.

Value Proposition: AI-powered eco-shoes made just for you – better fit, better world.
